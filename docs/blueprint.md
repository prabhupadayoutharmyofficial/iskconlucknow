# **App Name**: Divine Lucknow Darshan

## Core Features:

- Homepage & Navigation: Serve as the main entry point to the temple's online presence, featuring prominent links to all core sections and visually reflecting the temple's serene environment.
- Daily Arati & Darshan Timings: Dynamically display current and upcoming daily ritual schedules, allowing for easy updates and ensuring visitors have the most accurate information.
- Upcoming Events & Festivals Calendar: An interactive calendar showcasing upcoming temple festivals and events with dates, brief descriptions, and the ability to view event details.
- Temple Information & About Us: Comprehensive sections detailing the temple's rich history, its mission, the significance of the deities, and other relevant information.
- Photo Gallery: A curated display of high-quality photographs, categorized by temple views, events, and important moments, allowing visitors to experience the beauty of the temple.
- Visit & Contact Details: Essential information for visitors including the temple's address, contact numbers, email, operational hours, and simple directions.
- Daily Spiritual Reflection: An AI tool that generates a short, uplifting spiritual quote or message each day, relevant to Vaishnava philosophy and temple teachings, providing daily inspiration to visitors.

## Style Guidelines:

- A dark color scheme to evoke a sense of reverence and depth. Primary UI elements will utilize a warm golden-yellow (#E8BA30), reminiscent of saffron, for its spiritual significance and vibrance. The background will be a desaturated, deep warm brown-grey (#353127), providing a sophisticated base. An accent color, a brighter, softer yellow-orange (#F6CE55), will highlight key actions and interactive elements.
- For a blend of traditional elegance and modern readability, headlines will use 'Alegreya' (serif) for its elegant and intellectual feel, while body text will use 'Inter' (sans-serif) for its clarity and objective style, ensuring readability of longer informational texts.
- Icons will be vector-based, clean, and minimalist, drawing inspiration from traditional Indian spiritual symbols like the lotus or other Vaishnava motifs where appropriate, while general UI icons will maintain a simple, serene aesthetic.
- A clean, well-structured layout with clear content hierarchy, balancing ample white space with engaging imagery. A responsive design will ensure optimal viewing across various devices, employing multi-column layouts for richer content on larger screens and a streamlined single-column on smaller ones.
- Subtle and graceful animations will enhance the user experience without distracting from the serene content. This includes gentle fade-ins for content sections, smooth transitions for image galleries, and subtle hover effects on navigational elements.
